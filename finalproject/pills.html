Got Pills Poster Redesign

As a class assignment, I had to redesign a poster for a pill disposal drive 4 different ways. The general constraints for all 4 posters were black and white only and only minimal graphical elements comprised of basic shapes. The first poster only uses 1 typeface in only 1 size and 1 weight. The second is 2 sizes and 2 weights. The third could have used any size and weight and the final poster was created using up to 2 typefaces and any size and weight. Everything was created in Adobe Illustrator by me.
