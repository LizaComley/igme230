CherryBomb Improv Logo

I was approached by someone who needed designers for an "RIT meme themed t-shirt company". I created this graphic based off of some balloons that RIT places around campus every year, around the start of the fall semester.

The students at RIT always make fun of the balloons because the longer the balloons are up, the more stupid and for lack of better term, wonky, they look.

I was given the constraint that the design had to be made in a similar style to "Paper Mario" but other than that I was given full creative control. I used Adobe Illustrator to create the graphic.
