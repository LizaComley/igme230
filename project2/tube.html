3D "What If" Tube Project

In my 3D modeling class, my professor challenged me by asking the question, "What If?" He then told the class to create a tube that ponders that question. I decided to create a tube of sushi, because why not? We then made the tube into a series and I of course decided to make chopsticks in a tube as well. The tubes were created in Cinema4D and the ads' were made using Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator.
